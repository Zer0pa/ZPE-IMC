guide is a routing summary only and is not legal advice.

<p>
  <img src="../.github/assets/readme/section-bars/what-gets-a-fast-response.svg" alt="WHAT GETS A FAST RESPONSE" width="100%">
</p>
Reports with a reproducible case, an evidence path, or a specific
artifact reference. Vague questions without context will be asked
to provide more information before they are addressed.

<p>
  <img src="../.github/assets/readme/section-bars/what-does-not-get-a-response-here.svg" alt="WHAT DOES NOT GET A RESPONSE HERE" width="100%">
</p>
- Requests to modify the wave1.0 compatibility vector without a
  family coordination process
- Claims disputes without counter-evidence artifacts
- Questions already answered in `docs/README.md`,
  `docs/FAQ.md`, or `CONTRIBUTING.md` — check those first

<p>
  <img src="../.github/assets/readme/section-bars/response-expectations.svg" alt="RESPONSE EXPECTATIONS" width="100%">
</p>
This is a source-available project maintained by a small team.
We aim to acknowledge issues within 48 hours and triage within
7 days. Security reports are prioritised — see `SECURITY.md`.
